# Task 2 – Data Visualization and Storytelling

## Objective
Create a dashboard that tells a business story using sales data.

## Tool Used
- Power BI

## Dataset
- Superstore.csv

## Dashboard Features
- Total Sales KPI
- Total Profit KPI
- Sales Trend
- Sales by Category
- Profit by Sub-category
- Sales by Region
- Sales by Segment
- Discount vs Profit
- Interactive Filters

## Key Insights
- Technology generated the highest sales.
- The West region had the highest revenue.
- Some furniture products, such as Tables, had low or negative profit.
- Higher discounts were generally associated with lower profits.

## Business Recommendations
- Reduce excessive discounts on low-profit products.
- Increase focus on high-performing categories.
- Improve sales strategies in lower-performing regions.
